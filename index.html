Trevan McClure (aka "Sweet T"), born in Northridge Hospital on 4/20/1990, began his “ad-venture” into music long BEFORE he was even born. “My mom use to go to my dad’s concerts when she was pregnant with me. They were married to one of his songs, and I was born to his song ‘Spring’… Wait… Is it weird that I was born in the Spring to a song called Spring?”. “My musical career didn’t really take off until I decided… I’m… gonna bang… those pots and pans. I mean sure, what did I know, I wasn’t even a year old… I had no idea what I was getting myself into. Everyone knows… playing pots and pans only gets you so far… But I did it… and I’ve never looked back.”
